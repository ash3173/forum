    • Navigating the website.
    PGRKAM aims to create necessary framework to facilitate Wage and Self-employment for the unemployed people of the State of Punjab. Website provides personalized support to the people, to navigate and discover jobs which suite their interest and qualifications.


    • Homepage

      • This is the main interface of PGRKAM where user can navigate across all the available services, which include job search, chatbot assistance, resume builder, and contact us.




    • Chatbot Support

      • User finding difficulty to navigate through the website and attain services can chat with the AI assistance, which answers your domain specific questions in a personalized manner.




    • Categories

      • Filter your job search based on keywords,location or your desired category, with which you can avoid unnecessary job options.




    • Hiring through PGRKAM

      • Know how PGRKAM provides an interface between employers and employees, and improves employability through seminars and counsellings.




    • Job Listings

      • Navigate to this page to view job listings for both full-time and part-time positions.




    • Resume Builder

      • Improve your job search process by creating an effective resume that showcases your skills perfectly, using the in-app resume builder.




    • Contact Us

      • Visit this page to access contact information for any inquiries, and our administrators will be available to assist you.
